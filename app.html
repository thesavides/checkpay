Electronic Fund Transfer Disclosure

Before you submit your first check deposit, please review the following important information about your rights and protections under federal law.

Your deposit is an Electronic Fund Transfer

When you deposit a check through CheckPay, it is processed as an electronic fund transfer (EFT) to your account. This is governed by the Electronic Fund Transfer Act and Regulation E, which protect you.

Your rights include

  • Right to documentation of every transfer (electronic receipts)
  • Right to resolve errors within 60 days
  • Limited liability for unauthorized transfers ($50 if reported within 2 business days)
  • Right to save or print the full Deposit Terms for your records
